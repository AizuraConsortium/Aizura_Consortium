# AAIC Token Airdrop - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

---

## General Questions

### What is the AAIC Token Airdrop?
The AAIC Token Airdrop rewards community members with AAIC tokens based on their contributions and engagement. You earn points through various activities (social media connections, content creation, referrals, daily logins), and these points determine your share of the airdrop allocation.

### When will the airdrop happen?
The airdrop date will be announced at least 30 days in advance. Keep earning points until the snapshot date! Follow our official channels for announcements.

### How are tokens distributed?
Your token allocation is calculated as:
**Your Allocation = (Your Points / Total Community Points) × Total Airdrop Pool**

The more points you earn relative to others, the larger your share!

### Is there a minimum to receive tokens?
Yes, you must have at least **100 points** to qualify for the airdrop. This ensures only engaged community members participate.

### Can I participate from any country?
The airdrop is available globally, except in regions where cryptocurrency distributions are restricted. Please check your local regulations.

## Earning Points

### How do I earn points?
There are 5 main ways:

1. **Social Connections** (One-time): Connect Twitter (500), Discord (300), Telegram (200), GitHub (400)
2. **Referrals**: Invite friends (200 base + milestone bonuses)
3. **Content Creation**: Submit articles (1000), videos (800), threads (500), memes (300)
4. **Daily Engagement**: Login daily (10), weekly streaks (100), monthly streaks (500)
5. **Admin Bonuses**: Exceptional contributions rewarded manually

### What counts as a valid social connection?
Your social account must:
- Be at least 30 days old
- Have genuine activity (not a bot)
- Not be connected to another AAIC user's account
- Be successfully verified through OAuth

### Can I disconnect and reconnect social accounts?
No. Once you earn points for connecting a social account, disconnecting and reconnecting won't award additional points. You only get points once per platform.

### What happens if my social account gets suspended?
If your connected social account (Twitter, Discord, etc.) gets suspended or deleted, your earned points remain. However, you won't be able to reconnect the same platform for additional points.

## Referrals

### How do referrals work?
1. Share your unique referral link/code
2. Friends sign up using your link
3. You earn 200 points immediately
4. When they reach 1,000 points, you earn 500 bonus points
5. When they reach 5,000 points, you earn 1,000 bonus points

### Is there a referral limit?
No! You can refer unlimited friends. The more quality referrals, the more points you earn.

### What if my friend never becomes active?
You still keep the 200 base points. The milestone bonuses only trigger if they actively participate and earn enough points.

### Can I refer family members from the same household?
Yes, but be aware that our anti-Sybil detection monitors IP addresses. Multiple accounts from the same IP earning points rapidly may be flagged for review. As long as each person is genuinely engaging (not one person controlling multiple accounts), you're fine.

### Do I get points if my referral refers someone else?
No, referrals are only one level deep. If you refer Alice and Alice refers Bob, only Alice earns points from Bob's activity.

## Content Submissions

### What content can I submit?
Four types:
- **Articles** (1,000 pts): Blog posts, Medium articles (500+ words)
- **Videos** (800 pts): YouTube, TikTok, Instagram (2+ minutes)
- **Threads** (500 pts): Twitter/X threads (5+ tweets)
- **Memes** (300 pts): High-quality memes about AAIC

### What makes content approvable?
Your content must:
- Be original (not copied)
- Clearly mention or promote AAIC
- Be publicly accessible
- Meet quality standards (no spam/low-effort)
- Follow community guidelines

### How long does review take?
Most submissions are reviewed within 24-48 hours. During high-volume periods, it may take up to 72 hours.

### What if my submission is rejected?
You'll receive a notification with the reason. Common reasons:
- Content too short
- Not original (copied from elsewhere)
- AAIC not mentioned or barely mentioned
- Low quality or spam
- Inappropriate content

You can improve and resubmit.

### Can I submit the same content to multiple categories?
No. Each piece of content can only be submitted once. For example, you can't submit a YouTube video as both "video" and "article".

### Can admins award bonus points?
Yes! Admins can award 50-200% bonus points for exceptional content that significantly promotes AAIC or provides great value to the community.

### How many times can I submit content?
Maximum **1 submission per content type per day**. This prevents spam while allowing consistent contributors to accumulate points over time.

## Daily Engagement

### How does daily login work?
Simply visit the AAIC dashboard once per day (UTC timezone). You automatically earn 10 points per day logged in.

### What are streaks?
- **Weekly Streak**: Login 7 consecutive days → 100 bonus points
- **Monthly Streak**: Login 30 consecutive days → 500 bonus points

Streaks reset if you miss a day!

### What timezone is used for daily resets?
UTC (Coordinated Universal Time). Days reset at 00:00 UTC.

### What if I miss a day?
Your streak resets, but your accumulated points remain. Start a new streak the next day.

### Do I need to do anything besides logging in?
No. Simply visiting the dashboard counts as your daily login.

## Leaderboard & Rankings

### How often is the leaderboard updated?
The leaderboard updates in real-time. Your rank changes immediately when you or others earn points.

### Why did my rank go down even though I didn't lose points?
Other users earned points, moving ahead of you. Your points stay the same, but your relative position changed. Keep earning to move up!

### Is the leaderboard public?
Yes. Anyone can view the top 100 users and their point totals. However, we don't show wallet addresses or emails publicly.

### What information is shown on the leaderboard?
- Username or anonymous identifier
- Total points
- Rank position
- (Optionally) Country flag

Personal information like email and wallet addresses are private.

## Security & Fair Play

### How do you prevent cheating?
We use advanced anti-Sybil detection:
- IP address monitoring
- Behavioral pattern analysis
- Social graph analysis
- Referral chain detection
- Daily automated scans

Suspicious accounts are automatically flagged for admin review.

### What happens if I'm flagged?
If our system detects suspicious activity, your account is flagged for manual review. An admin will investigate, and if legitimate, the flag is cleared. If cheating is confirmed, you may be banned from earning additional points.

### Can banned users rejoin?
Bans are permanent for the current airdrop campaign. Banned users keep their existing points but cannot earn more. Future campaigns may allow reapplication.

### What activities are considered cheating?
- Creating multiple accounts
- Using bots or automation
- Submitting plagiarized content
- Circular referrals (A refers B, B refers A)
- Buying/selling referrals
- IP manipulation (VPNs to create fake accounts)

### I was flagged by mistake. What do I do?
Contact support at support@aizuraconsortium.com with your account details. Include any evidence that you're a legitimate user (links to your social profiles, content submissions, etc.). Appeals are typically reviewed within 5 business days.

## Account Management

### Can I change my email address?
Yes, through your account settings. Your points and referral code remain the same.

### Can I link a different wallet address?
Yes, you can update your wallet address anytime before the airdrop snapshot. The tokens will be sent to your most recent wallet address.

### What if I lose access to my account?
Contact support immediately. Provide proof of identity and account ownership. Account recovery may take 7-14 days for security reasons.

### Can I delete my account?
Yes, but **all your points will be lost**. There's no way to recover points after account deletion. Consider carefully before proceeding.

### Can I transfer my points to another account?
No. Points are non-transferable. Each user must earn their own points.

Yes. Contact support@aizuraconsortium.com with:

- The specific decision you're appealing
- Your explanation/evidence

Appeals are reviewed by senior admins within 5-7 business days.

## Technical Issues

### The website isn't loading. What should I do?
1. Clear your browser cache
2. Try a different browser
3. Check if your internet connection is stable
4. If still not working, check our status page or Discord for updates

### My points aren't updating after connecting social media.
1. Refresh the page
2. Wait 5-10 minutes (may be a delay)
3. Check if the social connection shows as "Verified"
4. If still no points after 1 hour, contact support

### I referred a friend but didn't get points.
1. Confirm your friend used your exact referral code
2. Check if your friend completed signup (not just visited the page)
3. Wait 10-15 minutes for the system to process
4. If still nothing, contact support with your referral code and friend's email

### My submitted content shows "Pending" for days.
During high-volume periods, reviews may take longer. If it's been more than 72 hours:
1. Check your email for any notifications
2. Verify your submission didn't violate any guidelines
3. Contact support if it's been 5+ days with no response
